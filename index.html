Heating/Cooling

  • While there is a natural-gas furnace, there is no built-in AC. You can close vents both in the garage/basement and in the rooms to direct where you want the heat flowing.
  • The sunroom is not insulated, so best to keep sliding doors shut at night to allow rest of house to stay warm/cool depending on the season.
  • There is a portable AC unit that works well in either of the upstairs bedrooms, as well as an evaporation fan that works well and uses 30% of the electricity of an AC. Additionally, you may choose to run the furnace fan while the heat is OFF to circulate the cool air from the basement/under the house which helps keep the entire house cool on hot days.
